Comparison of pharmacotherapies for obesity with sleeve gastrectomy: a network meta-analysis and systematic review.
Zachary Omeh, Tahira Khan, Olalekan Uthman, et al. — Expert review of endocrinology & metabolism Apr 2026
🏥 PopulationAdults with obesity comparing sleeve gastrectomy, tirzepatide, and other pharmacotherapies
💊 MedicationsTirzepatide (GLP-1RA/GIP agonist), sleeve gastrectomy, other pharmacotherapies
🌍 Country / DatasetMultiple (MEDLINE, EMBASE, Cochrane CENTRAL databases searched 2014-2024)
