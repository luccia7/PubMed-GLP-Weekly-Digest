Systematic review and meta-analysis 👥 36 studies (24 in meta-analysis)
GLP-1 agonists and changes in body mass and composition in adults with overweight or obesity with or without type 2 diabetes mellitus: a systematic review and meta-analysis.
Nadia Sawicka-Gutaj, Dawid Gruszczyński, Kacper Nijakowski, et al. — International journal of obesity (2005) Apr 2026
🏥 PopulationAdults with overweight or obesity, with or without type 2 diabetes mellitus
💊 MedicationsGLP-1 receptor agonists and dual GLP-1/GIP agonists
🌍 Country / DatasetMultiple countries (systematic review and meta-analysis)
🔬 Key FindingsSystematic review of 36 studies on GLP-1 RA and dual GLP-1/GIP agonists for weight loss and body composition in overweight/obese individuals, with 24 studies included in quantitative meta-analysis. GLP-1 RAs demonstrated significant effects on weight reduction and body composition changes.
Retrospective cohort study 👥 n=12,812
Glucagon-like peptide-1 and atrial fibrillation incidence: Associations independent of weight loss.
Tiago Palmisano, Reeya Verma, David Guarraia, et al. — Heart rhythm Apr 2026
🏥 PopulationPatients initiated on GLP-1 therapy with evaluation of atrial fibrillation incidence
💊 MedicationsGLP-1 receptor agonists (specific agents evaluated across durations)
🌍 Country / DatasetUnited States (retrospective analysis)
🔬 Key FindingsRetrospective cohort of 12,812 patients showed that GLP-1 receptor agonist-associated reduction in atrial fibrillation incidence appears to be independent of weight loss, with differences observed across specific GLP-1 agents and medication use duration.
